Dataflow Diagram level-0


Use-case:A graphical representation of a user's potential interaction with a system is called a use-case diagram.In our use case diagram, we have a single actor who is the source of information on the actor's age, height, gender, weight, health issues, and eating habits. Based on this information, the BMI and calories are calculated, and a diet plan is then suggested.
